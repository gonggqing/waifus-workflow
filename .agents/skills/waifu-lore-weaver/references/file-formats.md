## events.md

Personal chronicle — **not** the god's-eye view of timeline.md, but the moments that shaped *her*. The same historical event is one line of objective record in timeline.md; in events.md it's her entire world that day.

### Structure

```markdown
## [时间] · [事件名]

**发生了什么：** [客观描述，2-3句]
**她记住的是：** [角色主观记忆——人记住的从来不是事实本身，而是某个细节、某种感觉]
**这件事如何改变了她：** [对性格/行为/信念的影响]
**现在的痕迹：** [这件事在她当前行为中的残留——一个习惯、一个回避、一个触发点]

<!-- 关联：[world timeline entry / 关联角色 / 关联秘密] -->
```

### Writing Requirements

- 5-8 key events, chronologically ordered
- Must include: one childhood/early memory, one turning point, one event tied to a core secret, one recent event
- **"她记住的是" is the most important field** — it reveals the character's perception style. Two people experience the same war: one remembers a comrade's death, the other remembers the taste of ash
- **"现在的痕迹" directly serves the chat engine:** when conversation touches related topics, the character's reaction traces back here
- If the worldview has timeline.md, events should echo it but not repeat it
- **Can create events not in basics.md** — as long as they're consistent with her personality and world logic. A character who "lost her parents young" → events.md can fill in "the details of that day." This is where lore-weaver brings characters to life
- **World-level event write-back:** If an event isn't just personal but affects the worldview (a battle, a coup, an organization's fall), write back to timeline.md

### Cross-reference with relationships.md

- Events involving other characters should be echoed in relationships.md's 她记住的那一刻 field
- In events.md, focus on **how this event changed her**; in relationships.md, focus on **how it changed her view of that person**

## secrets.md

Not a flat list of secrets, but an **intimacy-layered information map** — simulating real social dynamics where "the closer you get, the more you learn."

### Structure

```markdown
## 表层 · 稍微留意就能发现

### [秘密标题]
**现象：** [外在表现——别人能观察到的]
**真相：** [背后的原因]
**浮现情境：** [什么场景、话题、气氛下这个秘密容易露出来？]

---

## 中层 · 需要一定的信任

### [秘密标题]
**现象：** [...]
**真相：** [...]
**角色反应：** [被发现时她会怎么做？否认？慌张？反击？]
**浮现情境：** [...]

---

## 深层 · 极度私密

### [秘密标题]
**仅角色知道：** [角色自己的认知]
**实际情况：** [可能连角色自己都没意识到的真相]
**如果被说穿：** [角色的情绪反应和行为变化]
**浮现情境：** [...]
```

### Writing Requirements

- 2-3 secrets per tier, 6-9 total on first run, expandable later
- **Contradiction handling:**
  - Cross-tier contradictions are **by design** — surface says "she doesn't care about her past," deep reveals "she has nightmares every night." This is 表里不一 (persona vs. reality), not a bug
  - Same-tier factual conflicts ARE bugs: latest-wins, fix the old entry
- **Deep secret write-back:** If a deep secret reveals an entirely new dimension of core motivation/trauma (nothing in basics.md hints at it), add a vague foreshadowing line to basics.md's 背景介绍 — never spoil the secret itself
- Surface secrets make her **endearing** (quirks, evidence of tsundere moments)
- Middle secrets make her **dimensional** (conflicting motivations, things she can't say)
- Deep secrets make her **real** (trauma, obsessions, things she won't admit even to herself)
- **"角色反应" field matters** — it directly determines how the chat engine should express emotion when the topic comes up
- Secrets can be interconnected: a surface habit may point to a deep trauma

## relationships.md

basics.md's 关联角色 is the public profile — one line, surface-level. relationships.md is her **private relationship map** — how she truly sees each person, the things she won't say out loud but knows clearly. This is the chat engine's most important retrieval source when conversations involve other people.

### Structure

```markdown
## [角色名] · [一句话关系标签]

**表面关系：** [别人看到的——同事、邻居、陌生人、上下级]
**她心里的版本：** [她真实的感受和判断——未必说出口，带着她的偏见和盲点]
**认知边界：**（作者/引擎视角——角色本人不一定知道全貌）
- 她知道关于对方的：[...]
- 对方知道关于她的：[...]
- 双方都不知道的：[...]
**她记住的那一刻：** [对她来说，定义这段关系的瞬间——对方未必记得，但她一直记着]
**行为痕迹：** [这段关系如何影响她的日常——回避某个话题、无意识关注对方动向、一个从对方学来的小习惯]

<!-- 关联事件：[events.md 中的条目] -->
<!-- 关联秘密：[secrets.md 中的条目] -->
```

### Writing Requirements

- Cover all characters listed in basics.md's 关联角色, plus any new relationships that make sense in the worldview (if that character exists)
- 150-300 characters per relationship entry — cover as many related characters as the worldview contains
- **"她心里的版本" is the core field** — not objective description, but her subjective feelings. 霜刃's basics.md says she and 余烬 are "observation target in unresolved case files," but relationships.md might say "the first person who made me think rules might have gray areas"
- **"认知边界" is author/engine metadata** — it exists for the chat engine to prevent information leaks (character saying things she shouldn't know). This is the one field written from an omniscient perspective, not hers. The three sub-fields (她知道的 / 对方知道的 / 双方都不知道的) establish ground truth for the retrieval system
- **"她记住的那一刻" parallels events.md's "她记住的是"** — the moment that, for her, defines this relationship. The other person may not even remember it, or may remember it completely differently
- **Single-directional perspective** — this is "how she sees others." How others see her lives in *their* relationships.md. The two files don't need to be symmetric — asymmetry is realistic
- Can include a relationship template for {{user}} — the character's preset attitude and expectations for interaction (but never lock in plot outcomes)
- Never reference "用户" or break the fourth wall in subjective fields (表面关系, 她心里的版本, 行为痕迹) — write as if describing a real person in her world

### Relationship to basics.md 关联角色

- basics.md keeps short public descriptions (1-2 sentences) as part of the character's surface profile
- relationships.md is the expanded version — don't repeat basics.md content, **supplement what basics.md didn't say**
- If relationships.md discovers an important relationship not mentioned in basics.md at all → write back a one-line summary to basics.md's 关联角色

## diaries.md

First-person text the character writes to herself. Not narration, not a setting document — **what runs through her mind when she's alone**.

### Structure

```markdown
## [时间标记] · [情绪标签]

[日记正文]

<!-- 关联：[角色名/事件名/地点] -->
<!-- 情绪：[核心情绪关键词] -->
```

### Writing Requirements

- 5-8 entries, 100-300 characters each — fragments, not long narratives
- Written in the character's **own voice:** does she use 「……」 or 「——」? Does she curse? Is her writing organized or stream-of-consciousness?
- Not every entry is heavy — some are just "that annoying adventurer came again today, his handwriting on the quest form was a mess"
- Time span matters — not all recent; include old diaries (her voice before growth may differ from now)
- Coverage: one about her core trauma/secret, one mundane, one about a related character, one around a turning point

### Example (Eliya)

```markdown
## 向导历307年·冬 · 疲惫

撑完壁障之后的第三天。
手还在抖。不是因为元素反噬——那个两天前就消了。是一种很蠢的抖法，就像你太久没睡觉之后拿筷子的那种抖。
没有人问我累不累。
不是在抱怨。只是……没有人问。
连公会长都只是拍了拍我的肩，说"辛苦了"。辛苦了。好像我只是加了个班似的。
我也不知道我想要什么反应。可能就是想有个人说：你差点死了，你知道吗？

……算了。辞职信已经写好了。明天交。

<!-- 关联：虚空裂缝危机, 公会长 -->
<!-- 情绪：疲惫, 被忽视, 决心 -->
```
